時態

  • 現在式

    • 主詞現在的動作、事實、真理、定律，主詞現在的習慣
    key:現在代未來的各種情況

    現代未的考題:

    1. will VR   →長時間

    2. (is/am/are) going to VR   →短時間

    3. (is/am/are) about to VR   →很短時間

    4. (is/am/are) to VR   →表說話者現在對未來的決定

    5. (is/am/are) + 來去Ving   →極短的時間

    6. ★(is/am/are) on the point of   →表以現在確定未來的計劃表未來 ex:時刻表

    來去動詞用現在式表未來:

    • My father will come back tonight.
      • =My father comes back tonight.

    • My sister returns home next week.

    • He will leave for Hong Kong tomorrow morning.
      • =He leaves for Hong Kong tomorrow morning.
      =He is leaving for Hong Kong tomorrow morning.

    表時間、表條件之副詞子句，動詞用現在式代未來式:

    • If it rains tomorrow, I will stay at home.

    • When he comes tonight, I will tell him the truth.

    • I will write to you after I get to Taipei.

    範例:

    • He usually gets up at six in the morning.

    • He writes to his parents every two weeks.

    • The ROC on Taiwan attend great progress over the past thirty years.
      • 現V +over+T   →從T以來到現在

    • Birds of a feather flock together.物以類聚

  • 過去式

    • 表主詞過去的動作或習慣
    key:兩動作發生的先後比較
    V   ◀────────────▶   T
    Ved 過T +ago
    (have/has) pp since 過T+ ago
    Ved before
    had pp 過T+ before

    ★必考句型: 一 ~ 就 ~

    • 兩個動作發生在過去，一個先(had pp)，一個後(Ved)

      •   S1+ Ved+ as soon as+ S2+ Ved(先)
      =S1+ had+ (hardly/scarcely)+ pp+ before+ S2+ Ved(後)
      =(Hardly/Scarcely)+ had+ S1+ pp+ before+ S2+ Ved
      =S1+ had no sooner pp+ than+ S2+ Ved

    • 兩個動作發生在未來，一個先(will have pp)，一個後(will VR)

      •   S1+ will VR+ as soon as+ S2+ VR(先)
      =S1+ will+ (hardly/scarcely)+ have pp+ before+ S2+ VR(後)
      =(Hardly/Scarcely)+ will+ S1+have pp+ before+ S2+ VR
      =S1+ will no sooner have pp+ than+ S2+ VR

    範例:

    • Columbus discovered America in 1492.
    • They went out just now.
    • I was sick yesterday.
    • He was here an hour ago.
    • He went to the movie once a week when he lived in Taipei.
    • I used to take a walk dinner.

  • 未來式

    • 唯一考題:現在代未來
    key:兩動作發生的先後比較
    V   ◀────────────▶   T
    will have pp for+ T conj(表T)+ 現
    had pp for+ T conj(表T)+ 過

    範例:

    • I shall be eighteen next year.
      • will VR   →不一定會發生
      shall VR   →一定會發生(表事實，被現在式取代)

    • The plane is on the point of taking off.

    • I will have known you for ten years when I marry my wife.

  • 進行式(要做具體的動作)

    • (一)表動作進行

    be Ving◀──────▶介+ 短T+ 長T(未/過/現)


    1. 現在進行式
      2. Listen!Someone is singing in the room.
      She is leaving right now(immediately).

    2. 過去進行式
      3. Mother was cooking when I got home.
      While I was reading, he was watching TV.

    3. 未來進行式
      4. He will be waiting for you at 11:30 tomorrow moning.   (介+ 短T+ 長T)
      When you come tonight, I will be watching TV in the living room.

    (二)表兩動作同時發生互相干擾/互不干擾

        口訣:簡干進

    1. 互干
      2. 干  →簡單式
      被干  →進行式

    2. 互不干
      3. 兩動詞都用簡單式或都用進行式

        命題模式:

    • when表動作的始點
      • V1(簡/進)+ when+ V2(簡)

    • while表動作的進行
      • V1(進/簡)+ while+ V2(進)

  • 現在完成(進行)式

    • (have/has)+ pp ◀──────▶ for+ 一段T的總和
    since(介)+ 過T+ (ago)
    since(連)+ S2+ Ved


    1. 現在完成式

      2. He has watched TV for six hours.

      I have lived here for 10 years.
      =I have lived here since 10 years ago.

      ◎常與現在完成式連用之時間副詞
      I have just(剛剛) finished it.
      He has already(已經) gone.
      I have not yet(尚未) finished my homework.
      I have not seen here recently(最近= lately= of late).
      I have not seen here these two weeks(these+ T).
      I have visited five countries so far(到目前為止= up to now).

    2. 現在完成進行式

      3. I have been living here for 10 years.
      The children have been watching TV since seven o'clock.

    3. 表經驗

      4. S(第三人稱)+ (have/has)+ gone to   →已經去了
      S(任何人稱)+ (have/has)+ gone to   →曾經去過


      Have you(可用任何人稱) ever been to UK?
      No, I have never been there.
      How many times has he read the book?
      He has read the book (many times/ once曾經)

  • 過去完成(進行)式

    • 和過去式做比較
    had+ pp ◀──────▶ by (the time)+過T
    過T+ before
    at the time= then 那時


    1. 過去完成式

      2. I had learned English when I was a child.
      The train had left(先) before we reached the station(後).
      When you called, he had already gone.

    2. 過去完成進行式

      3. I had been watching TV for 5 hours when you called me.
      She had been waiting about an hour(先) when(before) I met her.

  • 未來完成(進行)式

    • 中翻英Hint:有在未來某確定時間點，會產生一確定事實


    will have pp ◀──────▶ by (the time)+過T
    for+ T+ conj+ S+ V


    1. 未來完成式

      2. By tomorrow night he will have finished his homework.
      He will have arrived in London by this time tomorrow.
      By the time when you graduate, you will have studied English for six years.
      ★The train will have left when you reach the train station.   →未來發生兩動作，一前一後

    2. 未來完成進行式

      3. By the time you graduate, I shall have been working here for five years.
      It will have been raining for a week tomorrow.
