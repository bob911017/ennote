  • 主動vs被動

    • 一般V ↗ S(人) → 主 情緒V ↗ S(人) → 被
    ↘ S(事物) → 被 ↘ S(事物) → 主


    Example:
    • She opened the door.
      • =The door was opened by her.

    • He is writing a letter.
      • =A letter is being writen by him.

    • I have finished the job.
      • =I have been finished by me.

    • I shall have done my work when you come back.
      • =My work shall have been done by me when you come back.

  • 主動型表被動意

    • 發生

      • key:(1)本身無被動   (2)S為事物(非人)

      happen= occur= come about= break out= take place(發生/舉行)

      例外:hold(舉行)→ 被動→ S+ beV+ held

      Example:
      • The meeting will take place next year.
        • =The meeting will be held next year.

      • What happened to you.

      • World War I broke out in 1914.

    • S需要被...

      • V1,主 ↗ to V2,主R → V1 ↗ to be V2,被pp
      ↘ V2,主ing ↘ being V2,被pp


      S(事物)+ need
      want
      require
      deserve
      		 + V2ing
      to be V2


      Example:
      • My car needs to be washed.
        • =My car needs washing

      • My shoes want to be mended.
        • =My shoes want mending.

    • ★S值得被...

      • S(事物)+ be worth Ving
      S(事物)+ be worthy of being pp
      It is worthwhile to VR
      It is worthwhile Ving★
      It pays to VR★

      Example:
      • The book is worth reading again.
        • =The book is worthy to be read again.
        =The book is worthy of being reading again.
        =It is worthwhile to read the book again.
        =It is worthwhile reading the book again.
        =It pays to the book again.

      • Rome is worth visiting.
        • 羅馬值得一遊。

      • It pays to be honest.
        • 誠實不吃虧。

    • S位於...

      • stand= sit = lie= be located on= be situated in

      Example:
      • Our school stands on the hill.
        • =Our school is located on the hill.

      • Rome sits in Italy.
        • =Rome is situated in Italy.


      補充:
      visit .....拜訪地方
      call on ...拜訪人

    • 據說

      • They say that= People say that= It is said that= S+ be+ said to VR

      • People say that he is rich.
        • =It is said that he is rich.
        =He is said to be rich.

      • They say that jogging is good for health.
        • =It is said that jogging is good for health.
        =Jogging is said to be good be good for health.

      補充:
      • 對...有益
        • be good for sb.
        do good to sb.
        do sb. good

      • 對...有害
        • be bad for
        be harmful to
        be (harm/damage) to
        do sb. harm
