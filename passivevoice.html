  • 主動vs被動

    • 一般V ↗ S(人) → 主 情緒V ↗ S(人) → 被
    ↘ S(事物) → 被 ↘ S(事物) → 主


    範例:
      She opened the door.
      =The door was opened by her.

      He is writing a letter.
      =A letter is being writen by him.

      I have finished the job.
      =I have been finished by me.

      I shall have done my work when you come back.
      =My work shall have been done by me when you come back.
